Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch programs after starting?

Yes, you can switch programs within the first 2 weeks with no additional fees. Our advisors will help you transition smoothly.

Are the programs available internationally?

Yes, most of our programs are available globally through our online platform. Some in-person components may have location restrictions.

What if I can't attend live sessions?

All sessions are recorded and available for replay. You also get access to comprehensive study materials and can schedule one-on-one catch-up sessions.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for groups of 5 or more. Contact us for custom pricing for schools, organizations, or corporate teams.
