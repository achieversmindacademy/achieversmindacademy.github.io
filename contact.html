Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services

How quickly can I start a program?

Most programs have monthly start dates, and we can often accommodate special requests for immediate enrollment. Contact us to discuss your timeline and preferences.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for all our programs. You can choose from monthly, quarterly, or full payment options with no additional fees.

Can I attend programs remotely?

Absolutely! Most of our programs are available online with the same quality and interaction as in-person sessions. Hybrid options are also available for some programs.

What if I'm not satisfied with a program?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, all participants receive official certificates upon successful completion of our programs. These certificates are recognized by many educational institutions and employers.

Can you customize programs for organizations?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom programs for schools, universities, and corporate clients. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and requirements.
